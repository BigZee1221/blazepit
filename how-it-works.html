Step One

UNFOLD

Open the flat-pack. It clicks into position automatically. Zero tools, zero effort — just unfold and it's ready to stand.

02
Step Two

PLACE

Set on any stable surface. Ground, table, car hood, a flat rock — wherever you are. It levels itself on most surfaces.

03
Step Three

LOAD COAL

Add charcoal into the coal tray beneath the grate. We recommend hardwood lump charcoal for best heat and flavor. Fill about ¾ of the tray.

04
Step Four

LIGHT

Use fire starters or a lighter to ignite the coal. Let it build for 15–20 minutes until the coals are ashed over and glowing white-hot. Then you're ready.

Pro Tips

GRILL LIKE A PRO

Use Hardwood Charcoal

Hardwood lump charcoal burns hotter and cleaner than briquettes. You'll get better sear marks and real smoky flavor.

Wait for the Ash

Let coals ash over before grilling — about 15–20 minutes. You want them glowing white-hot, not flaming. That's when the heat is right.

Clean While Warm

Wipe the grate with a dry cloth while it's still warm for easiest cleanup. Let the grill cool completely before folding and packing.
